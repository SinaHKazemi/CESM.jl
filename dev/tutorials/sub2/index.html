To write a system of equations, use the aligned environment:

\[\begin{aligned} \nabla\cdot\mathbf{E} &= 4 \pi \rho \\ \nabla\cdot\mathbf{B} &= 0 \\ \nabla\times\mathbf{E} &= - \frac{1}{c} x_{Pin} \frac{\partial\mathbf{B}}{\partial t} \\ \nabla\times\mathbf{B} &= - \frac{1}{c} \left(4 \pi \mathbf{J} + \frac{\partial\mathbf{E}}{\partial t} \right) \end{aligned}\]

These are Maxwell's equations.
